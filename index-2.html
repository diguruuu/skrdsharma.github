Our Training Partner

Training forms the backbone of an efficient guarding force. Recognizing the need for efficient and continuous training as the core for maintaining process effectiveness, Fireball was instrumental in founding OSSIM, Orion School of Security & Intelligence and Management. OSSIM is an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Training Institute.

  • It is a state-of-the-art training insitute which is the  First Security Education and Training Institute in Asia, where training is provided for all levels of Personnel in every facet of the subject be it on land, sea, or air.
  • Best practices from the Armed and Police Forces across the globe have been incorporated in the training program.
  • Provides comprehensive training plan for Security Officers, Managers, and other Senior Security Personnel.
  • Certified trainers from the SSSDC with extensive experience in training security personnel from a cross-section of the industry. The trainers include Officers, JCOs, and PBORs from the Indian Armed Forces. Our trainers are enthusiastic, competent and well versed.


The training program addresses core topics such as security officer duties, asset protection, emergency evacuation procedures, ethical guidelines, and report writing. OSSIM has an elite panel of Security Industry experts and eminent educationalists on its Advisory Board. For more details, visit www.ossim.in
